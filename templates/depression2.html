Depression

A depression is defined as a large, organized group of thunderstorms with a low-pressure center capable of producing strong winds less than 31 kmph.

Damage Expected

Tropical cyclones classified as depressions are typically the weakest in terms of wind speeds and are not expected to cause significant damage. The associated storm surge and rainfall can cause localized flooding and some damage to weak structures, but typically not widespread destruction

Actions Suggested

Citizens dwelling near coastal areas are suggested to stay indoors and Fishermen are advised not to venture into the open seas.

Super Cyclone

A Super Cyclone is defined as a large, organized group of thunderstorms with a low-pressure center capable of producing strong winds greater than 222 kmph.

Damage Expected

Extensive structural damage to residential and industrial buildings. Total disruption of communication and power supply. Extensive damage to bridges causing large-scale disruption of rail and road traffic. Largescale flooding and inundation of sea water. Air full of flying debris.

Actions Suggested

Total suspension of fishing operations. Large-scale evacuation of coastal population. Total suspension of rail and road traffic in vulnerable areas. People in affected areas to remain indoors.
